Common questions from UK clients
Are you actually based in the UK?
Yes. Outhbox Limited is registered in England and Wales (Company No. 15622468). Our registered address is Unit F, Winston Business Park, Sheffield, S35 2PS. We operate remotely but are a legitimate UK-incorporated company with full legal accountability under UK law.
Do you work with small businesses or only larger companies?
Primarily small and growing businesses. Our pricing starts at £700 for a business website and scales up to custom enterprise software. Most of our UK clients are sole traders, small teams, and growing businesses that need proper technology without agency-level overhead.
How do payments work?
We invoice in GBP and accept bank transfer, Wise, and Stripe. Larger projects use a milestone structure — deposit to begin, staged payments tied to deliverables. We do not take large upfront payments before starting work.
Can you help with GDPR compliance?
Yes. All websites we build are built with GDPR considerations from the start — cookie notices, privacy policies, data handling. Our cybersecurity service also includes a GDPR compliance review as part of the audit process.
