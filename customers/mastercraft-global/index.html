The challenge

Mastercraft Global Ltd required a digital platform that could clearly articulate three distinct but connected service lines — coaching and personal development, creative direction, and Global Talent Endorsement advisory — without losing coherence or diluting the premium positioning the business demands. The site needed to serve individual clients and organisations simultaneously, with different entry points for each. No previous platform existed that could hold all three at the level of quality the brand required.

What Outhbox built

Outhbox designed and built mastercraftglobal.com as a structured, conversion-focused professional site. The architecture separates each service line clearly while maintaining a unified brand identity across all three pillars. The site includes individual service pages with specific calls to action, a founder profile section establishing credibility, and a project portfolio showcasing past work. The design language was developed in direct relation to yemilaja.co.uk — both sites serve clients who operate at the intersection of professional excellence and creative leadership, and both required brand-level visual coherence rather than generic template execution.

  • Full multi-service website with three distinct service line sections
  • Individual service pages with targeted calls to action
  • B2B and B2C entry points designed into the same architecture
  • Founder profile section establishing authority and credibility
  • Project portfolio with case study presentation
  • Brand-level visual coherence aligned with premium positioning
"Both mastercraftglobal.com and yemilaja.co.uk serve clients who operate at the intersection of professional excellence and creative leadership. Generic templates were not an option for either."
Outcomes

Mastercraft Global Ltd now operates with a digital platform appropriate to its positioning as a premium advisory business. The site supports new client acquisition across all three service lines and reflects the standard of work the business delivers to its own clients.

The platform is fully live and operational. It serves individual clients and organisations from the same coherent architecture, with each service line clearly accessible without diluting the others.
