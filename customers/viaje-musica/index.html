The challenge

Temitope Aina is a classical pianist whose work bridges European classical tradition and African musical heritage. Her Viaje Música concert series needed a dedicated digital home that matched the quality and intentionality of the performances themselves — not a generic events page, but a platform that could represent the artistic vision of the series, manage attendee registration across multiple concert editions, process payments securely, and communicate with ticket holders automatically. No such platform existed. Everything was handled manually.

What Outhbox built

Outhbox designed and built viajemusica.com as an immersive event platform structured around the concert series format. The site reflects the artistic character of the work — the intersection of European classical music and African heritage that defines Temitope’s performances. It includes dedicated pages for each edition of the concert series, with registration and payment processing integrated directly into each. Outhbox built automated email sequences to handle all attendee communication: registration confirmations, event reminders, and post-event follow-up. The platform was built to handle repeat usage across multiple concert editions without requiring a rebuild for each new event.

  • Immersive event website reflecting the artistic identity of the concert series
  • Dedicated pages for First, Second, and Third Edition concerts
  • Attendee registration integrated directly into each concert page
  • Stripe payment processing for secure ticket purchases
  • Automated registration confirmation emails
  • Event reminder sequences dispatched to registered attendees
  • Post-event follow-up automation
  • Platform architecture designed for reuse across future editions
"The platform was built to be reused — each new concert edition can be launched without rebuilding the underlying system. Temitope can focus on the music."
Outcomes

Viaje Música operates as a credible, self-sustaining event platform. Temitope Aina can promote new concert editions, manage registration, and communicate with attendees without operational overhead at the point of booking. Three editions have run through the platform.

The platform continues to support new concert editions as they are announced. The automated attendee communication system operates without manual intervention — from the moment a ticket is purchased to post-event follow-up.
