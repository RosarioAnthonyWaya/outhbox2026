The challenge

Yemi Laja operates across three entirely distinct professional identities: a performing artist with an active live event calendar, a transformational coach running structured programmes, and a Global Talent Visa consultant supporting high-achieving professionals seeking UK endorsement. She had no single digital home capable of representing all three, no system for capturing leads from visitors, and no automation to handle the volume of enquiries and event registrations her profile was generating. Everything was managed manually.

What Outhbox built

Outhbox designed and developed a full multi-section website at yemilaja.co.uk that consolidates all three professional dimensions into one coherent platform. The site includes a music and media portfolio, event pages with registration functionality, coaching and programme discovery pages, and a dedicated Global Talent Visa consultation section. Alongside the website, Outhbox built an automated lead management system. Visitors who express interest — whether through an event registration, a coaching enquiry, or a visa consultation request — are captured, tagged by intent, and entered into automated email sequences appropriate to their journey.

  • Multi-section website consolidating three professional identities
  • Event pages with live registration and ticketing functionality
  • Automated lead capture — tagged by intent and routed to the right sequence
  • Post-event follow-up emails triggered automatically
  • Coaching programme discovery pages
  • Global Talent Visa consultation section
"The platform currently supports Worship in Chaos — a live event running in London — with attendee communication managed automatically through the systems Outhbox built."
Outcomes

The platform currently supports an active event — Worship in Chaos, 2026 — with live ticketing and attendee communication managed through the automated systems Outhbox configured. Yemi can now operate all inbound interest across three business lines without manual intervention at the point of initial contact.

The client operates three distinct business lines from a single digital platform, with every lead captured, tagged, and followed up automatically. The system runs without daily administration.
