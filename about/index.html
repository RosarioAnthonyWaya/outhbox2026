What is Outhbox?

Outhbox is a UK–Nigeria technology company that builds the digital infrastructure businesses need to operate properly.

We design and develop websites, e-commerce platforms, automation systems, and custom software — all built to handle the real-world demands of running a business. Not demos. Not templates. Live, operational systems that work from day one.

We are UK-registered (Company No. 15622468) and operate across the UK and Nigeria, with a track record of projects from Sheffield to Lagos.
Outhbox exists because too many businesses are held back by systems that don’t work properly — websites that don’t convert, processes that rely on someone’s memory, and technology that’s more headache than asset.

We build the digital infrastructure businesses actually need to operate. Not demos. Not templates. Real platforms, real automation, real results. Whether you’re in Lagos or London, your business deserves technology that works.
Olawale Adeyemi
Founder & Director, Outhbox
Our Mission
Outhbox was founded to solve a recurring problem: businesses rely on technology they don’t fully control or understand.

Our mission is to help organisations run on systems that are:

  • secure by default
  • clearly structured and documented
  • monitored and supported continuously
  • built to scale without unnecessary complexity


When systems are reliable, teams can focus on decisions, customers, and execution — not firefighting.
Outhbox is led by professionals with hands-on experience in running and securing real systems.
