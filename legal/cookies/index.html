What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

How we use cookies

We use the following types of cookies on outhbox.com:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function. These include session cookies that keep you logged in and security cookies. They cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent, geographic region). This data is anonymised and aggregated. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Preference cookies — These remember choices you make on the site (such as language or region) to personalise your experience.

What we do not use

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any cookies that share your data with advertisers. We do not use social media tracking cookies.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies, delete existing cookies, and set preferences for certain websites. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

Third-party cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies on our behalf to collect anonymised usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page.
