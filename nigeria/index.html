Tambayoyi da amsoshi — Common questions from Nigerian clients
Kuna karbar biyan kuɗi cikin Naira? Do you accept Naira payments?
Yes. We invoice in NGN for Nigeria-based projects and accept bank transfer to Nigerian accounts, Flutterwave, and Paystack. We publish our Nigeria pricing in naira so there are no dollar-rate conversion surprises.
Can you integrate Flutterwave or Paystack into my website?
Yes — both are standard for any e-commerce or payment-accepting website we build for Nigerian clients. We also support bank transfer order flows for clients who receive orders via WhatsApp or phone and need those feeding into the same system.
Do you build WhatsApp automation for businesses?
Yes. WhatsApp Business API automation is a core part of what we offer. Lead capture from WhatsApp, automated replies, order processing, customer follow-ups, and notification systems that run on WhatsApp rather than email-only sequences.
Za ku iya gina wani abu da zai yi aiki da NEPA? Will your systems work with power interruptions?
Yes. Everything we build is cloud-hosted and works independently of local power. Your website, automation sequences, and customer communication systems run from our hosting infrastructure — they operate whether your generator is on or not.
